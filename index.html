Bachelors of Computer Applications(BCA)

BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) is a three-year undergraduate course that focuses on computer science and its applications. It equips students with programming, software development, database management, and networking skills. BCA offers a comprehensive understanding of computer systems, preparing graduates for diverse IT careers and higher studies in the field.
